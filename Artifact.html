Zoo Bags
What to have:
✔ Plastic Bottles
✔ Old Fabrics
✔ Metal Zips
✔ Fake Eyes.. etc
What to do:
1 Cut the bottom quaters of Plastic Bottles.
2 Attach a couple of bottoms with each other using metal zip.
3 Cut fabrics to create mouth and nose and paste fake eyes.
